Let It Out

Hi! Welcome to this not so social.

Why?

Because there are times that we just need to talk, because there are times when you want to say something without being judged, because sometimes you need to get angry or because maybe, you just want to tell something.

How?

It's simple: click on the button on the right and write what you want, just vent!
You can click on any card to read someone's story.
Use the language of your choice.

Please:

Do not share personal data, do not use real names of people, do not offend those who are reading.
Remember: this cannot be the solution to your difficulties, if you think you need help contact a specialist, he/she will surely be able to help you.
